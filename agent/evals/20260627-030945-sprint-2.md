# Sprint 2 - Taste & motion polish - Verdict

Driven by the taste audit `20260627-030201-taste-audit.md`. The audit confirmed
the site already reads warm, appetizing, and on-identity; it raised 2
blocks-shippable findings and several nice-to-haves.

## Blocks-shippable findings - resolved

1. **Sticky filter bar too translucent (cards ghosted through).** Fixed: the bar
   is now a solid `bg-paper` surface with a bottom border and soft shadow (was
   `bg-paper/85` + blur). Verified at 390px scrolled: zero ghosting of card text
   or photos through the bar.
2. **Mobile filter row clipped "Dinner/Lunch/Meal" with no scroll affordance.**
   Fixed: added a right-edge gradient fade (mobile only) so the row visibly
   hints it scrolls horizontally; the cut category now fades rather than
   hard-clipping. Verified at 390px.

## Nice-to-haves addressed

- **Empty detail lower half (highest-value).** Added a "More from the kitchen"
  section: 4 related dishes (same-category first, then filled from the rest),
  each a mini card linking to its detail page, plus a short warm line. The
  former cream void now reads as intentional and keeps people browsing.
  Verified on Khaman Dhokla (shows Rava Dokla, Khandvi, Masala Upma, Sabudana).

## Not changed (deliberately)

- **Filtered grid leaves a dead column with few results** and **portrait photos
  crop to background**: left as-is. Left-aligned grids are conventional, and
  per-image focal cropping needs per-dish focal data we do not have. Revisit if
  desired once the admin tool (Sprint 3) can capture a focal point per photo.
- **Zero-result empty state** is unreachable (every category has dishes); the
  deliberate message exists in code but cannot be exercised through the UI.

## Re-verification

- `pnpm build` exits 0 (19 SSG dish pages + index).
- Visual checks at 1280x900 and 390x844 confirm the fixes; no horizontal
  overflow; behavior (counts, filtering, gallery) unchanged from Sprint 1 PASS.

## Overall verdict: PASS
